This is a Parallax Site

Parallax scrolling is a trend where the background content is kept in place and the foreground content moves vertically while scrolling. The parallax effect was disabled for mobile devices as it can make a rough navigation and detract from the user experience. This site is experienced to its full potential on ipad and bigger devices (laptops, desktops)
